Key Insight

The relay network never sees your original text. It operates on noisy vector embeddings that are close enough to find meaningful matches but too lossy to reconstruct what you actually said. Progressive disclosure (category → detail → contact) ensures you control what you reveal, and when.

Design Principles

Private by Construction

Resonance doesn't protect your privacy with a policy document. It protects it with math, cryptography, and architecture decisions that make surveillance structurally impossible.

🛡

Private by Design

Three independent privacy layers: local-only AI embedding (Nomic-embed-text), Laplace differential privacy noise, and XSalsa20-Poly1305 end-to-end encryption. No server ever holds your raw data.

🔀

Complementary Matching

Resonance matches needs to offers, not duplicates. "I need a plumber" finds "I do plumbing." The system understands intent and reciprocity, not just keyword overlap.

🔓

No Account Required

Identity is a did:key keypair (Ed25519) generated locally on your device. No email, no phone number, no username, no password. You are your key.

💻

Consumer Hardware

14ms p95 embedding latency on CPU. No GPU required, no cloud dependency. The entire protocol runs on a laptop, a Raspberry Pi, or a phone.

🌐

Decentralized Relay Mesh

No central server. Relays are stateless and interchangeable. Anyone can run one. If a relay disappears, the network routes around it.

📜

Open Source

MIT licensed. TypeScript. Node.js 20+. Every line of code is auditable. 119 tests and 35 eval metrics ensure it works as documented.
